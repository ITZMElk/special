Maaf Karna Meri Pyari Behen ❤️

Tumhare bina yeh dil adhoora hai,
Maaf karna agar maine tumhe dukhi kiya hai...

Hamesha tumhara saath nibhaane ka waada karta hoon,
Bas meri yeh masoom si galti maaf kar do.

Tumhari muskaan meri duniya hai,
Tumhara gussa meri haar hai...

Waada karta hoon ab kabhi nahi dukhaunga tumhe,
Bas ek baar phir se muskara do.

Tumhara bhai,
Jo tumhe duniya se bhi zyada chahta hai...

Shukriya Meri Pyari Behen 🌸

Tumhare pyaar ke liye, tumhare sapno ke liye,
Tumhari har chhoti si muskaan ke liye...

Tum meri zindagi ki sabse khoobsurat kahani ho,
Tumhare bina yeh dil bechain ho jaata hai...

Hamesha yaad rakhna, chahe kitni bhi door chale jao,
Tumhara bhai tumhare liye hamesha yahi rahega...

Tumhare pyaar aur sahansheelta ke liye,
Ek baar phir se shukriya meri pyari behen ❤️
